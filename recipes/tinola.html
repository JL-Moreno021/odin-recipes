Tinolang Manok

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Black Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Knorr Chicken Cubes
  • Green Chili
  • Chili Leaves
  • Chayote Squash

Directions

  1. Saute garlic until brown, then onion
  2. Put the chicken and saute until the chicken turns white
  3. Add black pepper, soy sauce, and fish sauce for aroma and taste, then stir.
  4. Put 2 bowls of water, enough to soak the chicken. Let it simmer until tender.
  5. Add chayote squash on first boil, then green chili and chili leaves last.
  6. Serve in a bowl.
