Kalderetang Baboy

A Filipino tomato-based stew known for its rich, savory flavor and distinct hint of spice.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Black Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Pork
  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Liver Spread
  • Kaldereta Ready-Mix
  • Red Chili

Directions

  1. Saute garlic and onion
  2. Add the pork along with cheese and saute
  3. Add 1/2 cup of water to simmer the pork and boil.
  4. Add the carrot and potato on first boil.
  5. Add the caldereta ready-mix and boil.
  6. Add soy sauce and fish sauce for flavor and red bell pepper.
  7. Add the liver spread and red chili.
  8. Server with rice.
