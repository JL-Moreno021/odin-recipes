Chicken Adobo Recipe

Classic Filipino recipe, umami-rich flavor of chicken seared in oyster sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Dried Bay Leaf
  • Black Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Slice the onion and dice the garlic.
  2. Heat the pan, pour cooking oil, and saute the onion and garlic.
  3. Put chicken and saute for 2 mins.
  4. Add soy sauce and simmer until chicken is tender.
  5. Season with black pepper, and add sugar to balance the saltiness.
  6. Put bay leaf and a spoon of vinegar and let it simmer until the vinegar is cooked.
